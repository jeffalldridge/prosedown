# The Summit

The last hundred feet were hands and knees. Not dangerous — just steep enough that the body wanted all four points of contact. Elena pulled herself over the lip and stood up into the wind.

The world was enormous. Ranges behind ranges, each one lighter than the last, until the farthest peaks were indistinguishable from cloud.

She sat on the summit cairn and ate her sandwich. It was the best sandwich she had ever eaten.
